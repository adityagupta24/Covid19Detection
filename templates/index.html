Does X-RAY scans really help in detecting Covid-19 ..

The covid-19 virus can infect your respiratory tract and can cause irritation and inflammmation. Due to this reason, some traces of the virus are visible in the lungs which can be readily traced using X-RAY Scans. So using X-RAY Scans for detecting covid-19 is an alternative way.

According to some reasearches,it was published that CT/X-RAY Scans can detect coronavirus abnormalities before even the symptoms appear and can become a new mode to detect covid-19
